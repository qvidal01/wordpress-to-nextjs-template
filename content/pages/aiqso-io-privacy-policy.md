Privacy Policy

Last updated: [09/30/2025]

Who we are

Our website address is: https://aiqso.io.This site is operated by AIQSO.IO (AIQSO / Vidal Consulting).For privacy-related requests, you can contact us at: info@aiqso.io.

What personal data we collect and why we collect it

Comments

When visitors leave comments on the site, we collect the data shown in the comments form, along with the visitor’s IP address and browser user agent string to help with spam detection.An anonymized string created from your email address (a hash) may be sent to the Gravatar service to check if you are using it. The Gravatar service privacy policy is available at: https://automattic.com/privacy/.

Media

If you upload images to the website, you should avoid uploading images with embedded location data (EXIF GPS). Visitors can download and extract location data from images.

Contact forms

If you submit a contact form, we collect the information provided for customer service purposes. Submissions are retained for up to 6 months but are not used for marketing without your consent.

Cookies

If you leave a comment, you may opt-in to saving your name, email address, and website in cookies for convenience. These last for one year.

A temporary cookie is set on the login page to check if your browser accepts cookies (deleted when you close your browser).

Login cookies last two days, and screen preferences last one year. If “Remember Me” is selected, login persists for two weeks. If you log out, login cookies are removed.

If you edit or publish content, an additional cookie will be stored with no personal data, simply referencing the post ID. It expires after one day.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how our site is used. Google may set cookies and collect usage data. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by visiting: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.Google’s Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Embedded content from other websites

Articles on this site may include embedded content (videos, images, articles, etc.). Embedded content behaves exactly as if the visitor visited the originating website. These websites may collect data, use cookies, embed additional tracking, and monitor your interaction with that content.

Who we share your data with

Cloudflare – DNS and security services

Stripe – Payment processing

PayPal – Payment processing

Mailchimp (Intuit) – Email newsletters

Akismet Anti-spam – Spam detection

We do not sell or rent personal data.

How long we retain your data

Comments and metadata: retained indefinitely.

Registered user data: retained in the profile until deleted.

Contact form submissions: retained for 6 months.

Analytics data: retained for 1 year.

Payment transaction data: retained as legally required for accounting/tax purposes.

What rights you have over your data

If you have an account on this site, or left comments, you can request an exported file of the personal data we hold, including any data you provided. You may also request that we erase personal data, except for data we are obliged to keep for administrative, legal, or security purposes.

Where your data is sent

Visitor comments may be checked through Akismet Anti-spam.

Payment data is securely processed by Stripe and PayPal.

Data may be transferred outside the EU but safeguarded through standard contractual clauses and compliance with GDPR/CCPA.

How we protect your data

We use SSL encryption, restricted data access, and Cloudflare security services. Administrative accounts are protected with strong authentication and logging.

Data breach procedures

If a data breach occurs, we follow internal incident response procedures and will notify affected users as required by law.

Third-party data sources

We may receive anonymous technical data from hosting providers, Cloudflare, or analytics services to improve performance and security.

Automated decision-making and profiling

We do not use automated decision-making or profiling on AIQSO.IO.

Contact information

For privacy-related requests, please email: info@aiqso.io
